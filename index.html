Let's learn Japanese!

This site details a small selection of resources that help you to learn Japanese. The site provides a free kana chart to aid learning, and recommendations for websites and books to check out for learning the vocabulary, kanji and grammar.

There are many other more comprehensive lists and reviews of resources available elsewhere, but here I focus on the websites/web apps I use and the books I've bought/plan to use, having done a lot of research and tested out a number of different platforms.
